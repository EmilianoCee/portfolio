Career of Choice Research Essay

The Career of Choice Research Essay was a research writing assignment to gather information on the career I am most interested in pursuing. I chose to write about the career of a computer programmer because it is something I am passionate about. The purpose of the writing was to learn more about what I wanted to do in my future. Points researched include, career duties, work environment, education requirements, and salaries. Researching about the educational and experience requirements for becoming a computer programmer was a great help in understanding the steps I need to take to reach my goals. I chose to share this sample of writing because it shows my ability to research and write about a given topic.

Cover Letter: Fast & Friends Internship

The application cover letter for Fast and Friends Internship was a writing assignment for practicing cover letters. It was written to persuade the reader to consider me for an internship at a fictional design studio. I chose to showcase this writing because it shows my qualifications and enthusiasm for an opportunity to display my skills.
